Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Connor Hill's Blog

What is this blog about?

This blog focuses on iOS development, SwiftUI tutorials, app architecture patterns, and my journey building apps. I share practical tutorials, code examples, and insights from my experience as an iOS developer.

How often do you post new content?

I typically publish new tutorials and articles 1-2 times per month. Subscribe to get notified when I publish new posts!

Do I need an account to read articles?

No! All blog posts are free to read without an account. However, creating an account allows you to leave comments, get email notifications about new posts, and save your preferences.

How do I create an account?

Click on the Settings tab on the homepage and fill out the signup form with your name, email, and password. You'll be logged in immediately after creating your account.

Can I unsubscribe from email notifications?

Yes! Go to your account settings and adjust your email preferences. You can choose which types of emails you want to receive, or turn them all off.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click on "Forgot Password?" in the footer or use the password reset option in your account settings. We'll send you an email with instructions to reset your password.

How do I delete my account?

Log in and go to your account settings. Scroll down to the "Account Management" section where you'll find the option to delete your account. This will permanently remove your account, comments, and all associated data.

Can I share your articles?

Absolutely! Feel free to share links to any articles on social media, in your own blog posts, or with colleagues. I appreciate you spreading the word!

Do you accept guest posts?

Not at this time, but I'm always open to collaboration ideas. Feel free to reach out via email if you have something in mind!

How can I contact you?

You can reach me at hilljamesconnor@gmail.com. I read all emails and try to respond within a few days.

Are the code examples free to use?

Yes! All code snippets and examples in my tutorials are free to use in your own projects. No attribution required, though it's always appreciated!

Still have questions?

If your question isn't answered here, feel free to send me an email or leave a comment on any blog post. I'm happy to help!
